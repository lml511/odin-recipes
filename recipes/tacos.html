Tacos

Description

Tacos are a versatile Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients such as seasoned meats, beans, cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and salsa. They are often topped with fresh herbs, lime, and avocado, offering a vibrant mix of flavors and textures in each bite.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Ground beef, chicken, or fish
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salsa or pico de gallo

Steps

  1. Cook the Meat: Brown ground beef, chicken, or fish in a skillet with seasoning (like taco seasoning or spices) until fully cooked. Drain excess fat if needed.
  2. Prepare Toppings: Chop lettuce, dice tomatoes, shred cheese, and slice avocados. Make or prepare salsa, guacamole, and any other desired toppings.
  3. Warm Tortillas: Heat tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until soft and pliable.
  4. Assemble Tacos: Place cooked meat in the center of each tortilla. Add your choice of toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and salsa.
  5. Serve: Garnish with sour cream, avocado slices, lime wedges, and cilantro as desired. Enjoy!
