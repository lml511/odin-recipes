Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made of layered sheets of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's baked until bubbly and golden, offering a comforting blend of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or sausage
  • cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Set to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Prepare Meat Sauce: Cook ground beef or sausage with chopped onion and garlic in olive oil until browned. Add tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Cook Noodles: Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Mix Cheese Filling: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, an egg, and some Parmesan cheese.
  5. Assemble Lasagna: In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, place a layer of noodles on top, spread a layer of cheese mixture, and sprinkle with mozzarella. Repeat layers, ending with sauce and a generous layer of mozzarella.
  6. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  7. Cool and Serve: Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
